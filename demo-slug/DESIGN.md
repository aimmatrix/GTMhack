# Demo Slug Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

A sleek founder-demo animation for Noodle / Lightfern Reach: premium dark canvas, precise white product UI, warm coffee-shop example, and crisp sales motion. The slug should make the product purpose obvious in seconds: Noodle turns a target business name and sales goal into a research-backed outreach angle for Lightfern.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#000000`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Ink: `#202228`
- Canvas text: `#f6f3ea`
- Muted text: `#717680`
- Product accent: `#7b6cff`
- Espresso accent: `#4c3126`
- Fresh signal accent: `#2f7d5b`
- Line: `#e6e1d8`
- Dark line: `#202024`

## Typography

- Wordmark/display: `Instrument Serif`, Georgia, serif
- Interface/body: `Inter`, system-ui, sans-serif
- Data labels: `JetBrains Mono`, ui-monospace, monospace

## Motion

- Product UI moves with short, decisive 0.35-0.7s entrances.
- Use wipe transitions between story beats.
- Animate from stable end-state layouts.
- Keep text large enough for a screen-recorded demo.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic dark SaaS gradients or decorative blobs.
- Do not rely on tiny UI text for the key insight.
- Do not make the animation feel like a landing page hero.
- Do not add decorative blobs or one-note coffee-brown styling.
